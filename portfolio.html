Developed a sovereign, open-source runtime for building reliable AI workflows as deterministic state machines. Enables businesses to orchestrate LLMs, external tools, and business logic with full data control and vendor independence.

  • Challenge: Proprietary AI platforms lock users into vendor ecosystems and hide critical logic behind APIs
  • Solution: Vendor-agnostic runtime executing AI workflows as explicit, observable state machines with hooks for extensibility
