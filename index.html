Common Questions

Can you build a multi-tenant platform from scratch?

Yes. I design and implement multi-tenant SaaS/PaaS platforms, control planes, Kubernetes operators, self-service tooling, IaC, GitOps delivery, and the monitoring needed to operate them.

Do you handle the move onto the new platform?

Yes. I focus on the target platform, the onboarding path, and the operating model your team will use after handover.

What does "enablement" mean in practice?

Workshops, pairing, runbooks and best practices so your team owns the platform after I leave. The goal is a self-sufficient team, not a dependency on me.

Which clouds and tools?

AWS, Azure, GCP and IONOS; Terraform (incl. custom providers), Ansible, Kubernetes, ArgoCD/Flux, GitLab and GitHub Actions; Prometheus, Grafana, OpenTelemetry. Secure defaults throughout: secrets management, least-privilege, scanning, review gates.

Availability & engagement?

Remote-first from Germany, with occasional on-site when it helps delivery. Best fit: scoped SaaS/PaaS platform builds, Kubernetes control-plane work, assessments, and advisory retainers where implementation and handover matter. Contract details on request.
